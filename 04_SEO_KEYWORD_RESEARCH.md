- `how to store`
- `is it worth it`

## Cach phan tich SERP thu cong

1. Search keyword o incognito.
2. Ghi top 10 ket qua.
3. Danh dau loai site: publisher lon, Amazon, Reddit, YouTube, niche blog, ecommerce.
4. Neu top 10 toan publisher lon va ecommerce, can keyword dai hon.
5. Neu co forum/Reddit/YouTube/niche blog nho, co the co co hoi.
6. Doc 3 bai top dau va ghi gap: thieu anh that, thieu bang kich thuoc, khong noi cho can ho nho, khong so sanh theo use case.
7. Xac dinh cach bai cua minh co gia tri them.

## Prioritization formula

Dung diem 1-5:

```text
Priority Score = Buyer Intent + Low Competition + Product Fit + Content Differentiation + Testability
```

- `20-25`: P1, publish trong 30 ngay.
- `15-19`: P2, publish trong 60-90 ngay.
- `<15`: P3, chi dung neu can ho tro topical coverage.
