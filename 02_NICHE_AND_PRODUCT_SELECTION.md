# Task 02 - Niche And Product Selection

## Muc tieu

Chon mot sub-niche Home/Kitchen du de xay authority, co buyer intent, co commission kha, co kha nang tao noi dung that va khong qua rong.

## Input

- Thi truong: Amazon US.
- Category chinh: Home/Kitchen.
- Site content: tieng Anh.
- Nguon research lean-first: Amazon search, Google autosuggest, Google Trends, Reddit/Quora/public forums, YouTube, Google Sheets.

## Output

- 1 sub-niche chinh cho 90 ngay dau.
- 3-5 content pillars.
- 30-50 san pham tiem nang.
- 20 keyword/san pham uu tien dau tien.
- Product scorecard de quyet dinh co viet bai hay khong.

## Lua chon mac dinh cho 90 ngay dau

Neu chua co du lieu rieng, chon:

```text
Primary sub-niche: Small kitchen appliances for small spaces
Supporting pillars: kitchen organization, coffee gear, compact cleaning tools
```

Ly do:

- De nham audience ro: renters, apartment owners, small kitchens, dorms, first-home buyers.
- Nhieu keyword long-tail: `best compact air fryer for small kitchen`, `best coffee maker for small apartment`, `best under sink organizer for kitchen`.
- Co the tao content huu ich khong chi la review: setup guide, size guide, storage guide, cleaning guide.

Decision rules:

- `30+`: viet bai trong 30 ngay dau.
- `25-29`: dua vao backlog, can keyword validation.
- `<25`: bo qua hoac chi dung lam supporting product.

## Quy trinh research san pham

1. Vao Amazon, tim seed query: `small kitchen appliances`, `kitchen organization`, `coffee gear`, `compact cleaning tools`.
2. Ghi lai autosuggest va category filters.
3. Mo top products nhung khong copy review/rating/gia vao site; chi dung de hieu market va lay ASIN/link sau khi vao Associates.
4. Tim Google query voi modifier: `best`, `for small spaces`, `under $50`, `for apartment`, `review`, `vs`, `alternatives`.
5. Doc SERP de xem ai dang rank: publisher lon, niche sites, forum, Reddit, YouTube.
6. Neu SERP co forum/Reddit/YouTube/niche blog nho, co the la co hoi.
7. Cham diem product scorecard.
8. Chon 10 san pham dau tien.
