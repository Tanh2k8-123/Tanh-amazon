# Amazon Affiliate Home/Kitchen 2026 - Project Index

Bo tai lieu nay la playbook thuc chien de xay mot website Amazon Affiliate trong ngach Home/Kitchen, nham thi truong Amazon US. Tai lieu van hanh viet bang tieng Viet; noi dung publish tren site mac dinh viet bang tieng Anh.

Mac dinh chien luoc: zero-budget, SEO-first, compliance-first. Uu tien static site va hosting mien phi cho phep commercial/affiliate use; cong cu tra phi chi la tuy chon nang cap khi da co tin hieu tot.

## KPI tong 90 ngay

| Nhom KPI | Muc tieu toi thieu | Ghi chu |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Foundation | Website live tren static host phu hop affiliate/commercial use, mobile-friendly, co sitemap, robots.txt, trang About/Contact/Privacy/Disclosure | Khong gan link Amazon truoc khi disclosure san sang |
| Content | 20 bai source-ready, moi bai co intent ro rang va CTA hop le | Uu tien review co bang chung rieng |

## Nguyen tac van hanh

- Khong chay paid ads truc tiep den Amazon. Neu dung paid ads, traffic phai ve site cua minh va tuan thu chinh sach Amazon.
- Khong copy anh, gia, rating, review tu Amazon thu cong. Dung anh tu chup, anh duoc cap phep, cong cu Amazon hop le hoac Product Advertising API khi du dieu kien.
- Khong viet bai chi tong hop lai thong tin san pham. Moi bai can co goc nhin, tieu chi danh gia, bang chung, so sanh va loi khuyen mua/khong mua.
- Moi affiliate link can disclosure ro rang, gan voi noi dung khuyen nghi.
- Kiem tra lai dieu khoan Amazon truoc moi dot scale lon vi chinh sach co the thay doi.

## Definition of Done cho du an v1

- Da hoan thanh 10 task file tu 01 den 10.
- Co website live voi it nhat 5 bai dau tien.
- Co keyword backlog cho 30 bai tiep theo.
- Co SOP publish va tracking hang tuan.
- Co compliance checklist truoc khi dang ky Amazon Associates.
- Co quy trinh de chuyen tu blog-only sang blog + video khi co du noi dung.
