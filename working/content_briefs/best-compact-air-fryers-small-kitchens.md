- Basket shape affects what food fits.
- Cleaning burden can decide whether the product gets used.
- A tiny air fryer can be too small for two people.

## Evaluation criteria

- Footprint: width, depth, height, cabinet clearance.
- Capacity: realistic portions, not only quart rating.
- Cleaning: removable basket, coating, corners, drip areas.
- Storage: cord, shape, whether it can fit a cabinet.
- Heat and safety: rear/top vent clearance.
- Value: not price-copying; evaluate feature usefulness.
