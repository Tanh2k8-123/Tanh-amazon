# Brand Candidates - Tanh Corleone

## Brand decision

Recommended default:

```text
Tanh's Compact Kitchen Notes
```

Positioning:

```text
Practical buying notes, setup guides, and product comparisons for small kitchens, apartments, and compact homes.
```

Audience:

```text
Apartment renters, first-home buyers, small kitchen owners, dorm users, and people who want useful kitchen gear without wasting counter space.
```

## Shortlist

| Rank | Brand name | Why it works | Risk | Decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Tanh's Compact Kitchen Notes | Personal, clear niche, good for reviews and guides | Slightly long | Recommended |
| 2 | Tanh's Kitchen Notes | Personal and flexible, can expand beyond compact kitchens | Less specific for SEO positioning | Backup |
| 3 | Tanh's Small Kitchen Lab | Strong testing/review feel | "Lab" implies more first-hand testing than we may have early | Use later if testing heavy |
| 4 | Compact Kitchen by Tanh | Clear and concise | Less personal warmth | Good for social handle |
| 5 | Tanh's Apartment Kitchen | Clear audience | Narrower than Home/Kitchen | Good if focusing renters only |

## About page angle

Draft:

```text
Hi, I'm Tanh Corleone. I run Tanh's Compact Kitchen Notes to help people choose practical kitchen gear for apartments, small kitchens, and compact homes. The site focuses on space, usability, cleaning, storage, and value, not just product hype.
```

Editorial promise:

```text
Every guide explains who a product is for, who should skip it, and what trade-offs matter before buying.
```

## Brand rules

- Do not use Amazon, Kindle, Prime, or other trademarks in the brand/domain.
- Keep the tone practical, calm, and evidence-led.
- Do not overclaim first-hand testing until products are actually tested.
- Use `Tanh` as the personal voice; use `Tanh's Compact Kitchen Notes` as the site brand.
- Prefer simple visual identity: clean typography, warm neutral background, one accent color, product/use-case images.

## Decision

Use `Tanh's Compact Kitchen Notes` unless a deployment URL forces a shorter variant.
