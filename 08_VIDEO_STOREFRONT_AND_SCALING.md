# Task 08 - Video, Storefront And Scaling

## Muc tieu

Mo rong traffic va trust tu blog sang video/social, Amazon Influencer Storefront va Shoppable Videos khi website da co nen tang noi dung.

## Input

- Co 15+ bai blog hoac 5+ bai co tin hieu SEO.
- Co product shortlist va mot so san pham co the quay/chup.
- Co compliance/disclosure workflow.

## Output

- Video content plan.
- YouTube channel structure.
- Amazon Influencer readiness checklist.
- Storefront category plan.
- Scaling decision tree.

## Khi nao bat dau video

Bat dau video khi co it nhat mot trong cac dieu kien:

- Ban co the quay demo san pham that.
- Mot bai blog co impression/click tot va co the chuyen thanh video.
- San pham can visual proof: air fryer size, organizer fit, cleaning tool demo.
- Ban muon tao traffic social trong khi SEO con cham.

## Kenh video uu tien

| Kenh | Vai tro | Noi dung phu hop |
| --- | --- | --- |
| YouTube Shorts | Reach nhanh, test hook | 30-60s demo, before/after, quick comparison |
| YouTube long-form | Authority va search | Buying guide, product comparison, setup tour |
| TikTok/Instagram Reels | Discovery | Small kitchen hacks, product demos |
| Amazon Influencer videos | Onsite commerce | Short shoppable videos neu du dieu kien |

## Video formats cho Home/Kitchen

| Format | Vi du |
| --- | --- |
| Size demo | `Can this compact air fryer fit under a cabinet?` |
| Before/after | `Under-sink cabinet before and after organizer setup` |
| Comparison | `Mini food processor vs blender: which one saves more space?` |
| Mistakes | `3 mistakes when buying appliances for a small kitchen` |
| Cleaning test | `How hard is this air fryer basket to clean?` |
| Top picks | `3 apartment kitchen tools I would buy again` |

## SOP bien bai blog thanh video

1. Chon bai co buyer intent hoac impression tot.
2. Rut ra 1 promise duy nhat cho video.
3. Viet script 30-60s cho Short hoac 5-8 phut cho long-form.
4. Quay bang dien thoai, anh sang tu nhien, canh that.
5. Them disclosure trong description va noi trong video neu co recommendation/link.
6. Link ve bai blog thay vi direct Amazon neu muon giam compliance risk va tang site traffic.
7. Publish va ghi vao video log.
8. Embed video vao bai blog lien quan.

## Amazon Influencer readiness

Amazon Influencer Program rieng voi Associates va danh gia dua tren social presence/engagement. Khi co storefront active, influencer co the upload videos len Creator Hub; neu co 10 video duoc moderation approve va hoan tat onsite commission signup, videos co the xuat hien tren product detail pages lien quan.

Checklist san sang:

- [ ] Co social channel active.
- [ ] Co niche ro: small kitchens / compact home.
- [ ] Co video demo that, khong chi slideshow.
- [ ] Co disclosure tren social/video.
- [ ] Co 10+ video san pham chat luong de nop/duyet.
- [ ] Co Storefront category plan.

## Scaling with ads network

Ads network khong phai uu tien truoc khi co traffic. Tai thoi diem lap tai lieu, Mediavine main requirement la khoang `$5,000+ annual ad revenue`; Journey by Mediavine bat dau tu khoang `1K sessions`. Dung thong tin nay lam moc tham khao, nhung luon kiem tra lai nguon chinh thuc truoc khi apply.

Thu tu monetization:

1. Amazon Associates.
2. Other affiliate programs co commission cao hon neu phu hop.
3. Email list gui ve content tren site.
4. YouTube/social traffic.
5. Ads network khi traffic du va UX van tot.
6. Digital product nho: checklist, printable, buying planner neu co audience.

## Scaling decision tree

| Dieu kien | Hanh dong |
| --- | --- |
| Bai co impressions nhung chua click | Rewrite title/meta, them FAQ |
| Bai co click nhung khong affiliate click | Cai thien CTA, comparison table, product fit |
| Bai co affiliate clicks nhung khong sales | Xem lai product choice, price range, buyer intent |
| Topic blog co tin hieu tot | Lam video va supporting articles |
| Video co reach tot | Tao bai blog/hub tu video |
| Co 1K+ sessions/thang | Xem xet Journey/ads light, khong lam hong UX |
