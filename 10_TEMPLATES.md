## 1. Affiliate disclosure templates

### Site-wide Amazon statement

```text
As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.
```

### Dau bai viet

```text
Disclosure: This post contains affiliate links. If you buy through these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.
```

### Link-level label

```text
Paid link
```

Or:

```text
Amazon paid affiliate link - we may earn a commission.
```

Do not rely on `Amazon affiliate link` alone as the only link-level label.

### YouTube/social description

```text
Disclosure: Some links are affiliate links. If you buy through them, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.
```
