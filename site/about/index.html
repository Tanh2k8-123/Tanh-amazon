About Tanh's Compact Kitchen Notes

Practical kitchen gear notes for apartments, small spaces, and first homes.

Hi, I'm Tanh Corleone. I run this site to help people choose useful kitchen gear without filling every inch of counter and cabinet space.

The focus is simple: small kitchens need better trade-off thinking. A product can be popular and still be wrong for a tiny counter, a narrow cabinet, or a renter who moves often.

Editorial approach

Each guide explains who a product is for, who should skip it, and which trade-offs matter before buying. The review framework prioritizes footprint, cleaning, storage, build quality, and everyday usefulness.

When a product has not been personally tested, the article will say so. Research-based guides compare manufacturer specifications, documentation, size constraints, and practical small-space use cases. First-hand photos, measurements, and testing notes will be added as products are tested.

How the site earns money

This site may earn commissions from affiliate links. Affiliate relationships do not control the editorial conclusions. Products should earn the recommendation on usefulness, not payout.
