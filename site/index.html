How we choose

Every review starts with a small-space problem, then filters products by the trade-offs that matter in real kitchens.

Criterion Question What gets favored
Counter footprint Does it earn the space it takes? Tools that solve frequent jobs without staying in the way.
Cleaning burden Will someone actually clean it after a weekday meal? Fewer crevices, removable parts, and clear maintenance steps.
Storage fit Can it fit a cabinet, shelf, cart, or narrow counter? Stable dimensions, simple cords, and stackable parts.
Use case Who should buy it, and who should skip it? Specific recommendations instead of one-size-fits-all rankings.
