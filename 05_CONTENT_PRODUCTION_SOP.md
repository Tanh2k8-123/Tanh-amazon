# Task 05 - Content Production SOP

## Muc tieu

San xuat noi dung tieng Anh co kha nang rank va convert nhung van huu ich, minh bach, compliance-friendly. Moi bai can co goc nhin rieng, khong phai rewrite lai trang Amazon.

## Input

- Keyword tu [04_SEO_KEYWORD_RESEARCH.md](04_SEO_KEYWORD_RESEARCH.md).
- Product scorecard tu [02_NICHE_AND_PRODUCT_SELECTION.md](02_NICHE_AND_PRODUCT_SELECTION.md).
- Compliance checklist tu [01_COMPLIANCE_RESEARCH.md](01_COMPLIANCE_RESEARCH.md).

## Output

- Bai viet publish-ready bang tieng Anh.
- Content brief cho tung bai.
- Bang product comparison co tieu chi that.
- Link plan va CTA plan.
- Tracking row trong content log.

## Nguyen tac chat luong theo Google

Moi bai review nen:

- Evaluate from a user's perspective.
- Chung minh nguoi viet hieu san pham/chuyen de.
- Co bang chung: anh tu chup, video, do luong, link nguon, test notes neu co.
- Co measurements: size, capacity, noise, cleaning time, counter footprint, wattage.
- Giai thich san pham khac doi thu o dau.
- Noi ro san pham phu hop voi ai va khong phu hop voi ai.

## Evidence ladder

Uu tien bang chung theo thu tu:

1. Tu mua/test/chup anh/quay video.
2. Muon san pham hoac test tai cua hang/showroom.
3. Anh nha san xuat/media kit co quyen su dung.
4. Specs tu manufacturer/manual.
5. Pattern tu nguon cong khai, khong copy review/rating Amazon.

Neu bai chua co first-hand test, can co dong methodology ro:

```text
We have not personally tested every product on this list. We selected these picks by comparing manufacturer specifications, size constraints, cleaning requirements, use cases for small kitchens, and publicly available product documentation. We update this guide as we test more products firsthand.
```

## CTA va link rules

- CTA nen ro, khong gay ap luc.
- Khong noi discount/price neu khong cap nhat tu nguon hop le.
- Link text nen mo ta san pham: `Check availability on Amazon`.
- Mo link trong tab moi la tuy chon UX; quan trong hon la disclosure.
- Them `rel="sponsored"` hoac plugin SEO tuong duong cho affiliate links neu CMS ho tro.

## SOP viet mot bai

1. Chon keyword va content type.
2. Tao content brief bang template trong [10_TEMPLATES.md](10_TEMPLATES.md).
3. Phan tich top 5 SERP de tim content gap.
4. Chon 3-7 san pham dua tren product scorecard.
5. Thu thap specs tu nguon hop le.
6. Xac dinh tieu chi danh gia: size, capacity, cleaning, noise, durability, storage, value.
7. Viet outline.
8. Viet draft tieng Anh.
9. Them disclosure dau bai.
10. Them bang comparison khong copy gia/rating Amazon thu cong.
11. Them internal links den hub/supporting articles.
12. Kiem tra factual claims va compliance.
13. Viet title/meta description.
14. Publish hoac schedule.
15. Ghi vao content log.

## Checklist

Editorial checklist truoc khi publish:

- [ ] Keyword intent ro.
- [ ] Bai co cau tra loi nhanh o dau.
- [ ] Co disclosure truoc affiliate link dau tien.
- [ ] Co methodology.
- [ ] Co bang comparison voi tieu chi huu ich.
- [ ] Co nhan xet `best for` va `skip if`.
- [ ] Co alternatives.
- [ ] Khong copy Amazon price/rating/review/image.
- [ ] Co internal links.
- [ ] Co meta title va meta description.
- [ ] Co update date neu noi dung co the thay doi.
