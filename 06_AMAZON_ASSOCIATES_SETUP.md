# Task 06 - Amazon Associates Setup

## Muc tieu

Dang ky Amazon Associates dung thoi diem, cau hinh site/link/disclosure dung cach, va dat muc tieu 3 qualifying sales trong 180 ngay ma khong vi pham chinh sach.

## Input

- Website da live.
- Co it nhat 5-10 bai chat luong.
- Co trang About, Contact, Privacy Policy, Affiliate Disclosure.
- Co traffic/impressions dau tien tu Google Search Console hoac social.

## Output

- Amazon Associates account.
- Tracking ID cho site.
- Link workflow.
- Affiliate link log.
- Plan dat 3 qualifying sales trong 180 ngay.

## Khi nao nen dang ky

Khong nen dang ky ngay khi site rong. Nen dang ky khi co:

- 5-10 bai publish tot.
- Trang phap ly va disclosure day du.
- Site co cau truc ro va khong phai thin affiliate.
- Co mot it traffic hoac co plan traffic cu the.

Ly do: sau khi apply, ban co 180 ngay de tao qualifying sales; Amazon se review khi co 3 qualifying sales.

## Thong tin can chuan bi

- Website URL.
- Mo ta site bang tieng Anh.
- Nguon traffic du kien: SEO, organic social, YouTube.
- Category noi dung: Home, Kitchen, Home Improvement.
- Cach ban tao noi dung va gioi thieu san pham.
- Thong tin thanh toan va thue theo yeu cau Amazon.

## Mo ta site mau

```text
This website publishes practical buying guides, product comparisons, and how-to articles for people setting up small kitchens, apartments, and compact homes. We help readers compare kitchen appliances, organization tools, coffee gear, and cleaning products based on space constraints, usability, maintenance, and value.
```

## Cau hinh sau khi duoc vao dashboard

1. Tao Tracking ID rieng cho website.
2. Khai bao website/social properties dung theo yeu cau Amazon.
3. Lay link bang SiteStripe hoac cong cu link Amazon hop le.
4. Luu link vao affiliate link log.
5. Kiem tra moi link co Associate tag dung.
6. Them `rel="sponsored"` cho affiliate links neu CMS/plugin ho tro.
7. Kiem tra disclosure dau bai va footer statement.

## Affiliate link log columns

| Column | Mo ta |
| --- | --- |
| Date added | Ngay them link |
| Page URL | Bai viet tren site |
| Product name | Ten san pham noi bo |
| ASIN | ASIN neu co |
| Amazon URL | Link affiliate |
| Tracking ID | Tracking ID su dung |
| Link placement | Button, text, table |
| Disclosure checked | Yes/No |
| Last checked | Ngay check link gan nhat |
| Notes | Hien trang, can thay the, out of stock |

## Plan dat 3 qualifying sales trong 180 ngay

### Giai doan 1: ngay 1-30 sau apply

- Publish them 5 bai money/supporting content.
- Update 5 bai cu voi CTA va internal links tot hon.
- Chia se organic len Pinterest/YouTube Shorts/Reddit neu phu hop va khong spam.
- Tao 1 hub guide de gom internal links.

### Giai doan 2: ngay 31-90

- Publish 5-10 bai nua.
- Lam 3 video demo/summary neu co san pham test duoc.
- Gui email/social traffic ve bai viet tren site neu co audience opt-in.
- Review Search Console de tim query co impression nhung CTR thap.

### Giai doan 3: ngay 91-180

- Cap nhat bai co impression cao.
- Them comparison table, FAQ, better CTA.
- Tap trung vao keyword da co vi tri 8-30.
- Neu chua co sale, can audit conversion: link placement, intent mismatch, product choice, page speed, trust.

## Loi can tranh

- Tu mua qua link cua minh hoac nho nguoi quen mua lap lai de lay commission.
- Incentivize nguoi dung click/mua qua link.
- Chay ad truc tiep den Amazon.
- Dung trademark Amazon/Kindle/Prime trong domain, ad keyword, tracking ID.
- Cloak link gay hieu nham nguon dich.
- Copy price/rating/review/image tu Amazon thu cong.
- Dat affiliate links tren noi dung khong khai bao hoac medium khong co disclosure.
